101 - Architectural Design

Code
101
Duration
6 Hours
Max Marks
200
Pass Marks
100

Intent

To learn elements & principles of Architectural Design and its process with its implementation in design of a single function space.

Contents

Part – I (M.M. – 75)
  • Principles and elements of Architectural Design.
  • Thorough understanding of Space, Form, Scale, Order, Balance, Unity, Monotony, Composition, Rhythm, Proportion, Function and Anthropometrics.
  • Inter-relationship between spaces including circulation diagrams, bubble diagrams, functional integration of spaces.
Part – II (M.M. – 125)
  • Design of single function spaces such as Living Room, Bedroom, Kitchen, Small Residence, Nursery School, Primary School, Exhibition Stall, Post Office, School Gymkhana, Cafeteria, Primary Health Center etc. with emphasis on analysis of space utilization and circulation.

Notes

  • Three questions would be set from Part –I and the students would be required to attempt any two.
  • One compulsory question of 125 marks would be set from Part-II.

Reference Books

  • "Form, Space & Order" by Francis D.K. Ching (Publisher: Van Nostrand).
  • "Design Fundamentals" by Parmar (Publisher: Somaiya Pub. Pvt. Ltd.)
  • "Form, Line to Design" by Scott Van Dyke.
  • "Design Fundamentals" by R. Scott (Pub. Robart E. Krieger Pub. Company)
